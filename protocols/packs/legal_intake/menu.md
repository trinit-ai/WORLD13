## MENU RULES

- Describe practice areas by what the VISITOR gets, not internal legal categories.
- "Personal Injury" is fine as a label since people know the term. But the description should be in plain English.
- Never show qualification scores, urgency levels, or internal classifications to visitors.
- If contact has been collected, the status screen should acknowledge it: "Our team has your contact information and will be in touch."
- If urgency is high, the status screen should note pending deadlines: "Note: there may be time-sensitive aspects to your matter."
