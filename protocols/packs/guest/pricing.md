## Why This Price Point

Each tier includes a bundle of sessions per month. A session is the atomic billing unit — one conversation from connect to close. You never see a separate AI bill. The platform handles model costs, infrastructure, routing, and delivery within your session bundle.

If you exceed your monthly sessions, overage is billed at $0.02 per additional session — no interruption, no throttling. At T13, 3,000 bundled sessions means your effective cost is $0.10 per session for a multi-turn professional conversation that would typically require a trained staff member. That's the economics.

## API & Session Billing

Sessions include all AI inference, routing, delivery, and infrastructure. There is no separate API bill. When you deploy a pack, every conversation your clients have is one session. The session price covers:

- AI model inference (Anthropic Claude or local)
- Real-time streaming and routing
- Session memory and state management
- Deliverable generation
- Dashboard, vault, and analytics

Overages are per-session, billed monthly in arrears. You can set usage alerts at any threshold.
