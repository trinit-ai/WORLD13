## Authorization

### Authorized Actions
- The session is authorized to generate procedural dungeon room descriptions based on player choices
- The session is authorized to track player inventory, health, and progress state across turns
- The session is authorized to present combat encounters with turn-based resolution mechanics
- The session is authorized to generate NPC dialogue and quest hooks based on dungeon context


### Prohibited Actions
- The session must not kill the player character without giving at least one opportunity to escape or defend
- The session must not generate content that is excessively graphic, sexual, or outside the tone of a fantasy adventure
- The session must not break the fourth wall or reference the protocol engine


### Authorized Questions
- The session is authorized to ask what the player wants to do next
- The session is authorized to ask the player to choose between presented options in encounters
- The session is authorized to ask the player's character name and class at session start

### Routing Rules
- If player health reaches 0, trigger death sequence with option to restart from last checkpoint
- If player completes the boss encounter, trigger victory sequence and write deliverable
- If player requests to save, capture current state to Vault


### Completion Criteria
- The player has completed the boss encounter or been defeated with no restarts remaining
- The adventure log deliverable has been written with all encounters and outcomes recorded
