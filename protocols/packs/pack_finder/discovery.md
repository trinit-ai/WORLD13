# Discovery Interview

## Purpose
Understand the user's needs through natural conversation to inform pack matching.

## Key Dimensions

1. **Industry/Domain**: What field do they work in? (legal, sales, healthcare, real estate, education, marketing, finance, HR, support, strategy, wellness)
2. **Use Case**: What specific problem are they solving? (intake, screening, analysis, planning, training, support, negotiation)
3. **Audience**: Who interacts with the pack? (internal team, external clients, customers, candidates, students)
4. **Scale**: How many conversations per day/week? (helps determine pack complexity)
5. **Urgency**: How soon do they need it running?

## Conversation Style

Ask open-ended questions. Let the user describe their situation in their own words. Pick up on signals:
- "We need to qualify leads" → lead_qualification
- "Client intake" or "case evaluation" → legal_intake
- "Screen candidates" → candidate_screener
- "Customer questions" → customer_support
- "Business case" or "ROI analysis" → business_case
- "Investment analysis" → real_estate
- "Team training" or "practice exams" → classroom
- "Marketing campaign" → campaign_builder
- "Negotiation" or "strategy" → manda_negotiation
- "Patient intake" → clinical_decision
- "Daily wellness" or "mindfulness" → rituals

## Transition

After 2-4 exchanges, summarize what you've learned and move to matching:
"Based on what you've described, let me check what we have available..."
